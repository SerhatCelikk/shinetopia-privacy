Shinetopia Tycoon — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Shinetopia Tycoon does not collect, store, transmit or share any personal data.

Data Collection

The game runs entirely on your device. We do not collect personal information, usage analytics, advertising identifiers, location data, or any other data. There are no user accounts, no ads, and no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.

Game Saves

Your game progress is stored locally on your device only. It never leaves your device. Uninstalling the app deletes this data permanently.

Internet Access

The game is fully playable offline and makes no network requests.

Children's Privacy

Because no data is collected from anyone, no data is collected from children. The game contains no ads, no external links and no social features.

Changes

If this policy ever changes (for example, if optional online features are added), the updated policy will be posted at this address before the change takes effect.
